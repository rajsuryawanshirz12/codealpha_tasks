Air Jordan 1 Retro: The Icon That Started It All

Raj S. Jan 20, 2026

The Air Jordan 1 is more than just a shoe; it's a cultural icon. This high-top design in the classic 'Green' colorway is a testament to the timeless legacy that started it all.

A Legacy Began

Debuting in 1985, the Air Jordan 1 was banned by the NBA for breaking uniform rules, a move that only fueled its rebellious appeal. Michael Jordan wore them anyway, facing fines with every game, and the rest is history.

The 'Green' Colorway

This specific iteration features a rich green and white color blocking that is both bold and versatile. The premium leather construction and classic wings logo on the ankle collar stay true to the original design that changed the sneaker world forever.

How to Style

The beauty of the AJ1 High is its versatility. Pair it with baggy cargo pants for a streetwear look, or with straight-leg jeans and a vintage tee for a classic, timeless vibe.
