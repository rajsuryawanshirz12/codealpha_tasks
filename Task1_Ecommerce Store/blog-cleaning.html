How to Keep Your White Kicks Fresh

Sole Doctor Jan 12, 2026

There is nothing worse than stepping out in fresh white Air Forces and getting them scuffed immediately. Here is our expert routine for maintaining that factory-white shine.

1. Prevention is Key

Before you even wear them, spray them with a stain repellent like Crep Protect or Reshoevn8r. This creates an invisible barrier against liquids and dirt.

2. The Toothbrush Method

For midsoles, nothing beats an old toothbrush and some warm soapy water. Scrub in circular motions to lift dirt out of the textured rubber.

3. Laces Matter

Dirty laces ruin the whole look. Take them out and soak them in a bowl of warm water and bleach for 30 minutes. They will come out looking brand new.
