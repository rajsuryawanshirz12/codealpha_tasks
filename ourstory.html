The Mission

Why We Started

Finding authentic, limited-edition sneakers in India used to be a gamble. Fake pairs, insane import duties, and unreliable sellers made the culture inaccessible.

We decided to change that. DropZone was created with a simple promise: 100% Verified Authentic, shipped directly from Pune to your doorstep with zero hidden fees.
